Team Cytron

M6Web R&D team

About us

Team Cytron is one of the M6Web R&D teams initially in charge of french web sites m6.fr, w9.fr, 6ter.fr, paris-premiere.fr and teva.fr.

We are also closely taking part in the implementation of the second screen package for the M6 Group channels.

Technologies

We mainly develop REST APIs in PHP, MySQL and Redis by using Symfony, FOSRestBundle and Doctrine.

We pays close attention to the quality of our developments by making unit tests with atoum and functional tests with VigoJS.
